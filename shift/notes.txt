*** A note about types ***
In Go, byte is an alias for uint8. Go takes bytes as kind of whatever. -5 or 5 doesn't matter, it's a byte.
Rust isn't so free wheeling. There is no "byte" type. It's u8. And that's a STRICT u8, no negatives.
